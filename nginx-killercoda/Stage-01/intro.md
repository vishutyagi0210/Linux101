### What is Nginx?

Nginx (pronounced "engine-x") is a high-performance web server, reverse proxy, and load balancer. It powers some of the world's largest websites including Netflix, Airbnb, and WordPress.com.

### Why Nginx?
- **Speed**: Event-driven architecture handles thousands of connections efficiently
- **Reliability**: Battle-tested in production environments
- **Flexibility**: Web server, reverse proxy, load balancer, and more
- **Low Resource Usage**: Minimal memory footprint
